Books I've Read

Volumes: A Reading Collection by Clauie

Le Passager - Patrick Senecal
Rating: ★★★★☆
Summary: A gripping psychological thriller that kept me on edge!
The Whisper Man - Alex North
Rating: ★★★★★
Summary: A widower, Tom, moves his young son, Jake, to the town of Featherbank for a fresh start. The town is still haunted by a 20y.o serial killer case known as "The Whisper Man".
The Only One Left - Riley Sager
Rating: ★★★★☆
Summary: A caregiver named Kit who is assigned to look after Lenora Hope, a woman accused of murdering her family in 1929
Verity - Colleen Hoover
Rating: ★★★★☆
Summary: A psychological thriller about struggling write Lowen Ashleigh, who is hired to ghostwrite the rest of bestselling author Verity Crawford's book series after Verity is incapacitated in an accident.
Beach Read - Emily Henry
Rating: ★★★★☆
Summary: Romance about two rival writers, January and Augustus, who strike a deal to overcome writer's block by writing in each other's genres.
People We Meet on Vacation - Emily Henry
Rating: ★★★★☆
Summary: Two bestfriends, the spontaneous travel writer Poppy and the steady, home-loving teacher Alex, whose friendship is strained after a disastrous vacation to Croatia.
It Happened One Summer - Tessa Bailey
Rating: ★★★★☆
Summary: Romance novel about a wealthy LA socialite, Piper, who is sent to a small Washington fishing town to run her late father's bar as a lesson in responsibility.
The Seven Husbands of Evelyn Hugo - Taylor Jenkins
Rating: ★★★★☆
Summary: Reclusive Hollywood legend, Evenlyn, who chooses an unknown reporter, Monique, to write her life story.
The Woman in The Window - A.J Finn
Rating: ★★★★☆
Summary: Follows the life of Dr. Anna Fox who suffers from agoraphobia and lives a Reclusive life at her large home in New York City, where she one day witnesses a murder across the street.
The Night Swim - Megan Goldin
Rating: ★★★★☆
Summary: Triller about Rachel, who becomes entangled in a decades-old mystery while investigating a new case in a small town.
None of This is True - Lisa Jewell
Rating: ★★★★☆
Summary: psychological thriller about a popular podcaster, Alix, who meets Josie on their 45th birthday. As Alix delves deeper, she discovers dark and dangerous secrets.
Brother - Ania Ahlborn
Rating: ★★★★☆
Summary: 19-year-old Michael Morrow, who wants to escape his family's disturbing traditions in the Appalachian Mountains, where they engage in horrific activities.
