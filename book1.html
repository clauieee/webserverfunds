Summary & Reviews

This book was recommended to me by my sister and my interest in thriller books started with this one.

This novel follows a young man Etienne who lost his memories from before he was eight because of an accident that occured during his childhood.

He starts a new job teaching fantasy litterature and during his new commuting journey from the university to his Montreal apartment, he meets a hitchhiker, Alex.

Alex claims to be his childhood friend and begins to help Etienne recall his forgotten memories.

Fast paced book -- I read it in one sitting.
